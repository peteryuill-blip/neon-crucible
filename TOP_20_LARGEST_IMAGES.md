## Optimization Tips

1. **Use WebP format** - 25-35% smaller than JPEG at same quality
2. **Resize to max display size** - Most screens don't need images larger than 1920px wide
3. **Quality 80-85%** - Visually lossless for artwork photography
4. **Tools:** Squoosh.app (free, browser-based), ImageOptim (Mac), or Photoshop "Save for Web"
5. **Keep originals** - Store full-res versions separately as archival copies
